About
Utkarsh

Utkarsh: Feel the Rush
Utkarsh, the annual fest of Babu Banarasi Das Educational Group, is a celebration of creativity, innovation, and youth. A vibrant tapestry of cultural performances, exhilarating competitions, and intellectual pursuits, Utkarsh provides a unique platform for students to showcase their talents, explore their passions, and connect with the wider community.
Over the years, Utkarsh has evolved into a much-anticipated event, attracting students from across the region. It fosters a spirit of camaraderie, encourages healthy competition, and provides a valuable opportunity for personal and professional growth.
